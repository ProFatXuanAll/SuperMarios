What is Super Mario

Created by Nintendo,

Super Mario is a video game featuring their mascot, Mario.

Player follows Mario's adventures in Mushroom Kingdom,

Run up hills and jump across platforms,

Step on enemies and beat up evil Bowser,

Then finally rescue kidnapped Princess Peach.

What is Super Marios

Super Marios is a web realtime MMOG.

Using Mario to take on an exciting adventure,

Other real world Marios will attack you from EVERYWHERE.

Also, there are monsters blocking your way!

No matter what comes to you, just remember,

Accessories will always be there to your assistence.

Strive for the one and only glory!
