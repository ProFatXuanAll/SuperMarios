What is Mario

Super Mario is a series of platform video games created by Nintendo featuring their mascot, Mario. Alternatively called the Super Mario Bros. The Super Mario games follow Mario's adventures in the fictional Mushroom Kingdom. The player runs and jumps across platforms and atop enemies in themed levels. The games have simple plots, typically with Mario rescuing the kidnapped Princess Peach from the primary antagonist, Bowser.

What is Marios

Basicly same as Mario, but with multiple players play at the same time. Try to rescue Princess Peach and work with/fight over(?) other Marios!
